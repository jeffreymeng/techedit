Broken Login Url's

When you are linked to a login page, some data, including the link to go to after you login, is stored in the url. Sometimes, this link can become broken, causing you to get a message saying you are already logged in, but not redirecting you to any other pages. This is rare, but when it does happen, you can click on the My Collection link in the navbar, or on the link provided in the login page.
